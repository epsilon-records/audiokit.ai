# Vozz Rich - Electronic Press Kit

## Artist Overview

Vozz Rich is a dynamic US-based producer and DJ with roots in Mexico and California. His unique sound blends elements of Detroit house, underground raves, salsa, techno, and big room, creating an irresistible fusion that captivates audiences. With notable collaborations and remixes under his belt, Vozz Rich is an artist on the rise in the electronic music scene.

# 🎤 Artist Overview
Vozz Rich is a dynamic US-based producer and DJ crafting an infectious fusion of house music that draws from diverse influences including Detroit house, underground rave culture, salsa, techno, and big room sounds. With roots in Mexico and California, his unique cultural background infuses his productions with an authentic and distinctive flavor. His recent collaborations with artists like Kicevski, L25, and Gabriel Robella showcase his versatility and growing presence in the electronic music scene.

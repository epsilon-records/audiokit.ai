# 🎤 Artist Overview  
Vozz Rich is a dynamic producer and DJ with roots in Mexico and California, seamlessly blending influences from Detroit house, underground raves, salsa, techno, and big room sounds. With over 4,100 Spotify followers and a growing fanbase, Vozz Rich’s music is a vibrant fusion of cultural beats and energetic rhythms that ignite dance floors worldwide. His recent releases, including collaborations with artists like L25 and Franz Kolo, showcase his versatility and passion for creating unforgettable music. 🌍🎶

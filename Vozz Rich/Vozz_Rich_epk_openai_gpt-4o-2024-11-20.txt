# 🎤 Artist Overview  
Vozz Rich is a dynamic and captivating electronic music producer and DJ with deep roots in Mexico and California. With a unique ability to blend Detroit house, underground rave culture, salsa rhythms, and big-room energy, Vozz Rich delivers electrifying tracks that resonate across global dance floors. Recent collaborations with rising stars like Franz Kolo and remixes for notable names such as Jaytor and Kelly Reverb have elevated his profile. Vozz Rich’s creative direction bridges cultures and genres, ensuring his music is both innovative and unifying. 🎶

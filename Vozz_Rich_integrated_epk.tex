\section{\emoji{microphone} Artist Overview}
Vozz Rich is a dynamic electronic music producer and DJ whose signature sound masterfully fuses Detroit house, underground rave culture, salsa, techno, and big room elements. With roots in Mexico and California, his multicultural background infuses his productions with a unique cross-cultural appeal that sets him apart in the electronic music scene. His recent collaborations with artists like L25, Kicevski, and Franz Kolo, combined with standout remixes, showcase his versatility and growing influence in the industry.

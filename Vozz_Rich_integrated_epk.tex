# 🎤 Artist Overview  

Vozz Rich is a groundbreaking US-based electronic music producer and DJ whose sound is deeply rooted in his Mexican and Californian heritage.🌟 Blending the rhythmic pulse of Detroit house, the raw energy of underground rave culture, and vibrant Latin influences, Vozz Rich creates a unique cross-cultural electronic music experience. With a growing discography of high-energy tracks and remixes, he has collaborated with notable artists like Kicevski, L25, and Artsychoke, solidifying his presence in the global dance music scene. His innovative approach to fusing genres makes him a standout artist, continually pushing boundaries and igniting dance floors worldwide.

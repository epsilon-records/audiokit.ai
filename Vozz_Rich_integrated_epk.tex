\section*{\textcolor{primary}{Artist Overview}}
Vozz Rich (USA/Mexico/California) is reshaping electronic music through a fusion of Detroit house, underground rave culture, salsa rhythms, and big-room energy. Celebrated for genre-blurring productions and electrifying performances, his work has garnered recognition from \textbf{DJ Mag} and \textbf{Mixmag} for innovative cultural synthesis in electronic music.

# 🎤 Artist Overview
Vozz Rich is an innovative US-based electronic music producer and DJ who masterfully blends Detroit house, underground rave culture, salsa, techno, and big room elements into his signature sound. With roots in Mexico and California, his multicultural background infuses his productions with a unique cross-cultural appeal. His recent collaborations with artists like Kicevski, L25, and Franz Kolo showcase his versatility and growing influence in the electronic music scene.

# 🎤 Artist Overview  
Vozz Rich is a dynamic producer and DJ whose sound is a fusion of Detroit house, underground raves, salsa, techno, and big room beats. With roots in Mexico and California, Vozz Rich brings a multicultural energy to his music, creating tracks that are both electrifying and soulful. Over the past year, he has released a cascade of hits, collaborated with artists like L25 and Kicevski, and delivered remixes that have gained traction in the electronic music scene. His ability to blend diverse influences into cohesive, dance-floor-ready tracks has earned him a growing fanbase and critical acclaim.

# 🎤 Artist Overview

Vozz Rich is an innovative US-based electronic music producer and DJ crafting a distinctive fusion of Detroit house, underground rave culture, and Latin influences. With roots in Mexico and California, his sound seamlessly blends energetic house and dance music with cultural elements, creating an electric cross-cultural experience. His recent collaborations with artists like Kicevski, Franz Kolo, and L25 showcase his versatility and growing influence in the electronic music scene.

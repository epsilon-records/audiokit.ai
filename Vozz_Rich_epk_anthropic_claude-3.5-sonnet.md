# 🎤 Artist Overview

Vozz Rich is an emerging electronic music producer and DJ bringing a unique fusion of Detroit house, underground rave culture, and Latin influences to the dance music scene. Based in the US with roots in Mexico and California, his signature sound seamlessly blends house, techno, and big room elements with occasional salsa influences, creating an infectious cross-cultural dance experience. With a rapidly growing catalogue of original productions and remixes on major platforms, Vozz Rich is positioning himself as an exciting new voice in electronic music.

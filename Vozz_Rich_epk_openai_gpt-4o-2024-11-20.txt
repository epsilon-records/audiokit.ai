# 🎤 Artist Overview

Vozz Rich, a dynamic producer and DJ, captivates audiences with an eclectic mix of electronic beats. Drawing inspiration from Detroit house, underground raves, salsa, and techno, Vozz creates a rich, immersive sound. With roots in Mexico and California, his music bridges global influences into dancefloor anthems. Vozz Rich has made waves with vibrant tracks like "Breathless" featuring L25 and infectious remixes such as Artsychoke's "Butterflies." Whether crafting original hits or remixing, his artistry resonates across the electronic music spectrum.

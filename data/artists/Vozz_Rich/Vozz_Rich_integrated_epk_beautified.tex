\section*{\emoji{microphone} Artist Overview}
Vozz Rich is a dynamic electronic music producer and DJ whose signature sound fuses Detroit house, underground rave culture, and Latin influences into an electrifying musical experience. With roots in Mexico and California, he masterfully blends big room elements with underground house sensibilities, creating a distinctive cross-cultural sonic palette. His collaborative works with artists like L25, Franz Kolo, and PAUL PARKER have established him as an innovative force in the electronic music scene, known for energizing dance floors worldwide with his unique fusion of styles.

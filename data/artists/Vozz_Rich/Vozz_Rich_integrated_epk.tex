\section*{🎤 Artist Overview}
Vozz Rich is a dynamic electronic music producer and DJ whose signature sound bridges underground and commercial dance music. With roots in Mexico and California, he crafts an innovative fusion of house, techno, salsa, and big room influences. His multicultural approach to production has garnered attention through notable collaborations with L25, Artsychoke, and Jaytor, establishing him as an emerging force in the electronic music landscape. Vozz Rich's ability to seamlessly blend Detroit house, Latin rhythms, and contemporary dance elements creates an infectious cross-cultural experience that resonates on dancefloors worldwide.

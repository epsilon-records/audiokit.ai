# 🎤 Artist Overview

Vozz Rich, a US-based producer and DJ, combines an eclectic blend of sounds inspired by his Mexican and Californian roots. His music seamlessly fuses Detroit house, underground raves, salsa, techno, and big room into a unique electronic dance experience. With notable collaborations and remixes, Vozz Rich is carving out a distinctive space in the electronic music scene, captivating audiences with his vibrant and dynamic performances.

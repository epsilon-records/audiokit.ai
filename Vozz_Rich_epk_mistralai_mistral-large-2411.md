# 🎤 Artist Overview

Vozz Rich, a dynamic producer and DJ, seamlessly blends the diverse sounds of Detroit house, underground raves, salsa, techno, and big room into an electrifying mix. With roots in both Mexico and California, his music bridges cultural influences, creating a sonic experience that is as inclusive as it is captivating. Vozz Rich's productions and DJ sets are known for their high-energy beats and emotive melodies, making him a standout artist in the electronic music scene. His versatility and authenticity have earned him collaborations with notable artists and several high-profile remix projects.

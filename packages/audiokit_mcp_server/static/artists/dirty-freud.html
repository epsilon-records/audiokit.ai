Dirty Freud Electronic Press Kit (EPK)

Artist Overview

Name: Dirty Freud
Total Releases: 45
Genres: Electronic, House, Other Electronic Music

Dirty Freud is an electronic live & recording artist who has found success since moving to the North of England. Their music is a blend of Live Electronica wrapped in Jazz melodies, performed across the UK and Europe, gaining support from industry icons.

Career Highlights

  • Played headline slots at UK festivals including Glastonbury, All Points East, Citadel, Boomtown, Beatherder.
  • Received key support from Jon More of Coldcut and Ninja Tune, Rough Trade, and Keep It Dirty Records.
  • Praised by Nemone of BBC 6 Music as "Wonderfully Wonky" and heralded as "The Real Deal" by Don Letts of BBC 6 Music.

# 🎤 Artist Overview

Vozz Rich, a US-based producer and DJ with dynamic roots in Mexico and California, electrifies the electronic music scene. Fusing Detroit house, underground rave energy, salsa, and techno influences, Vozz creates a unique soundscape that captivates global audiences. Known for his vibrant house and big room productions, Vozz has dropped standout tracks like "Back To The Beat" and collaborated with artists such as L25 and Frans Kolo. His remix work on hits like "Butterflies" and "Dance All Night" showcases his ability to elevate tracks with an infectious energy and distinct personal touch.

# 🎤 Artist Overview: Vozz Rich

🎵 **Vozz Rich** is a dynamic US-based DJ and producer with roots in Mexico and California, blending an eclectic mix of **Detroit house**, underground rave culture, salsa, techno, and modern big-room vibes. His music resonates with both underground dance floors and global listeners alike. Vozz Rich's artistry has attracted attention through collaborations with talents like **L25, Artsychoke, and Franz Kolo**, and his remixes for notable tracks like _"Butterflies"_ and _"Dance All Night"_. With a pulse on the electronic scene and a style truly his own, Vozz Rich is emerging as a name to watch.

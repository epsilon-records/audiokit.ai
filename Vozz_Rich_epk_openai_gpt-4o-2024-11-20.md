# 🎤 Artist Overview

Hailing from the US with roots in Mexico and California, **Vozz Rich** is a genre-defying DJ and producer celebrated for blending the rhythmic pulse of Detroit house with the energy of underground raves, salsa, and big room techno. A master of fusing global influences, Vozz Rich crafts electrifying tracks that ignite dance floors worldwide. Notable highlights include collaborations and remixes with **Artsychoke**, **Jaytor**, and **Kelly Reverb**, showcasing his versatility and ability to command diverse electronic subgenres.
